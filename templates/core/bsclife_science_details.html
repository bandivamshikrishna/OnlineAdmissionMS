Bsc.life science details:

Bsc.life science stands for"Bachelor of Science in Life Science"

BSc Life Science is a 3-year undergraduate course involving the study of various life processes in plants, animals and organisms. The course, incorporating both practical and theoretical knowledge, was introduced in the academic year 2005-2006.

Principal subjects included in this course are Plant Biology, Biochemistry, Food Science, Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, Agricultural Science, Molecular Biology, Botany, Zoology, and Chemistry..

Duration: 3 years/6 semesters.

Eligibility: 10+2 in Science Stream.
